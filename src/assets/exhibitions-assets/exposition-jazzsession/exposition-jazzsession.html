Victor de Boo is a successful Dutch jazzdrummer and graphic artist. When you see his art it’s clear he mainly gets his inspiration from his daily work as a musician. At his exhibition ‘JAZZSESSION’ you find some of his art he made between 2017 and 2018 which are announcements for the weekly notorious and probably the longest running jazzsession in Holland (39 years) in Café De seed-scss in Amsterdam, a place where he plays each sunday since 1994.

The artworks are available in a limited signed edition in Hahnemuhle Fine Art Prints 50 x 50 cm € 175.– incl. frame | 25 limited editions.
